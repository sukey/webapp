People at a demonstration can tag tweets with #sukey, or visit http://www.sukey.io/ to anonymously send photo and text reports.

Verified information is then digested, structured, and fed back to demonstrators.


No smartphone? No problem! Text 'follow sukeysms' to 86444 to receive updates via SMS. Signup costs 10p and following texts are free. Please help others around you to get Sukey updates by showing them this!

▲

Sukey is a platform designed to keep people safe, mobile, and informed during demonstrations. We crowdsource and verify information from Twitter and other online and offline sources to provide demonstrators with a timely overview of what is happening on the ground.
